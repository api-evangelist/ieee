Many IEEE members who collect historical engineering artifacts often offer them to the IEEE History and Heritage group, which includes the IEEE History Center , to display. To bring these artifacts to the public, the group created the IEEE Global Museum , which curates traveling exhibits for display at conferences and in libraries, universities, and other venues.
